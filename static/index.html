Ultimate Seat Selector

This site allows for students and instructors to interactively choose a seat in a lecture. Once entering the password you will be moved to the seat selection page. Here you will click on a seat, which may be a physical one or you can click the virtual button if that is how you wish to attend. Once you click on a seat you will enter in your preferred name and last name to the text box. Note, only one per seat unless it is the virtual student area.
